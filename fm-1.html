FM-1

A compact and self-contained FM synthesizer.

Specs

  • 6 voices
  • 3 operators
  • ADSR envelope per voice
  • Sine-wave LFO per voice
  • 4 potentiometers and one rotary encoder
  • 17-key keyboard with mechanical keyboard switches
  • 1.3-inch OLED
  • Mono output
